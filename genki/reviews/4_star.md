Case Successfully Resolved Thanks to Persistence and Support
I’m updating this review to reflect how the situation was ultimately resolved.

Initially, I was extremely frustrated with how my claim was handled. As a Ukrainian refugee living in Portugal under the Temporary Protection Program, I bought travel insurance before my trip to Bali. A few days after arrival, I developed acute bronchopneumonia and required urgent medical care.

At first, my claim was rejected by DR-WALTER due to a 14-day waiting period. Their reasoning was that my home country was listed as Ukraine, and therefore they considered the policy to be taken “from abroad.” Despite providing documents that I was living in Portugal and purchased the policy there, the case was closed.

However, thanks to the support from Pedro at Genki, I was able to update my home country to Portugal retroactively. With the corrected certificate, I appealed again — and this time, DR-WALTER accepted the claim and reimbursed me in full.

While the process took several rounds of communication and was emotionally exhausting at times, I truly appreciate that Genki stood by me and helped escalate my case. Mistakes happen, but what matters is how a company responds — and in this case, they followed through.

So in the end, what started as a very negative experience turned into a fair outcome.
Thank you again to Pedro and the support teams at both Genki and DR-WALTER.
