Why choose SIP Medical Family Office?
SIP Medical Family Office is the world's leading independent international health insurance and health management consultant based in Zurich, Switzerland.

Our additional services at no extra cost

We will take care of you not only until you have found the right insurance solution - but for a lifetime.
