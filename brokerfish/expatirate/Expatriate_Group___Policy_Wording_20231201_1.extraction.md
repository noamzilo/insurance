The following words have the same meaning wherever they appear in this Policy Wording. No words in this Policy Wording are gender specific and are applicable to both male and female subjects.

from a Nurse, Physician or Specialist, including the issuing of any
medication or prescription.

<a id='60ad8d59-9179-4794-b867-47a50a7069ac'></a>

<a id='a7096629-29f9-4d50-b951-6bd72b3069c7'></a>

Company/Our/Us/We - Expatriate Group acting on behalf of certain
Underwriters, including any organisation appointed by Us to act on
Our behalf.

<a id='1d3f6f3e-58e2-406e-9e5e-16702238957f'></a>

<a id='f287a159-7434-4f34-8a92-b0425575c1d6'></a>

<a id='39b3ad03-b901-4de9-8eb9-0cee36843f1f'></a>

<a id='991ab1cf-9527-4ba6-a123-336bf378067d'></a>

the Period of Cover.
